Sarah's Applesauce

Description

This applesauce is delicious! I make it for my younger brother all the time and he loves it - and he hates canned applesauce!

Ingredients

  • 4 apples - peeled, cored and chopped
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, combine apples, water, sugar, and cinnamon.
  2. Cover, and cook over medium hear for 15 to 20 minutes, or until apples are soft.
  3. Allow to cool, then mash with a fork or potato masher.
